Frequently Asked Questions

What is escrow?

Escrow is a secure third-party service that holds funds until both buyer and seller fulfill their agreement.

How long does it take?

Once both parties agree, we can hold and release funds within minutes or hours depending on the deal.

What if there's a dispute?

We review the evidence provided by both parties and release funds fairly, or bring in an arbitrator for complex cases.

What payment methods do you accept?

We support major crypto wallets and bank transfers. Details are shared individually per transaction.
