How It Works

  1. Agreement: Buyer and seller agree on terms.
  2. Payment: Buyer sends funds to Trust Hold Pay.
  3. Delivery: Seller delivers the product or service.
  4. Verification: Buyer confirms the transaction is complete.
  5. Release: Funds are released to the seller.
